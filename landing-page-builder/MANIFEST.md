## 🎯 Core Features

### 6 Aesthetic Directions

1. **Minimalist** — Clean, professional, trustworthy
   - Colors: Black, gray, blue
   - Fonts: Inter, Helvetica Neue (sans-serif)
   - Vibe: Modern, simple

2. **Maximalist** — Bold, vibrant, energetic
   - Colors: Magenta, cyan, yellow on dark
   - Fonts: Poppins (bold sans-serif)
   - Vibe: Eye-catching, energetic

3. **Retro-Futuristic** — Edgy, neon, 80s/90s vibes
   - Colors: Neon pink, cyan, yellow
   - Fonts: Orbitron, Space Mono (monospace)
   - Vibe: Futuristic, bold

4. **Organic** — Natural, warm, approachable
   - Colors: Greens, browns, earth tones
   - Fonts: Playfair Display, Lato (serif + sans-serif)
   - Vibe: Warm, natural

5. **Editorial** — Magazine-style, sophisticated
   - Colors: Black, red, white
   - Fonts: Georgia, Droid Serif (serif)
   - Vibe: Premium, sophisticated

6. **Brutalist** — Raw, authentic, no-nonsense
   - Colors: Black and white
   - Fonts: IBM Plex Mono (monospace)
   - Vibe: Technical, honest

### 11 Essential Elements

Every landing page includes:

1. **Hero Section** — Compelling headline + CTA
2. **Social Proof** — Logos, badges, testimonials
3. **Problem Statement** — Validate visitor's pain
4. **Solution Highlight** — Why you're the answer
5. **Key Benefits** — 3-5 main reasons
6. **Feature Breakdown** — How it works
7. **Customer Testimonials** — Real quotes
8. **Case Study/Results** — Proof with numbers
9. **FAQ Section** — Answer objections
10. **Comparison Table** — (Optional) vs alternatives
11. **Final CTA** — Clear, urgent action button
